Our Story

How we met

We met in 2012 through a meetup group, Triad Twenty-Somethings. Kristen set up a bowling event for the group and Bryan attended. He was one of the first people to arrive and we spent the majority of the night talking, even in the parking lot after everyone was done bowling for the evening. When Kristen left, she knew she wanted to get to know Bryan better. We hung out again together with the group that following weekend, then Bryan asked Kristen on our first date that evening. We started dating on July 1, 2012. We decided to move in together and moved to Mount Airy, where we still live.

Bryan and Kristen took a trip to Duck, NC in May 2015 to celebrate Kristen's graduation from school and to spend a week together. We always try to have a week just to ourselves every year. One morning, Bryan asked if Kristen wanted to go watch and take pictures of the sunrise. It was chilly and windy that morning, but absolutely gorgeous. Bryan asked Kristen to close her eyes. When she opened them and turned around, he was on one knee with a ring in hand. On the sand was a heart with “Will you marry me?” written inside it. Of course, she said yes!

The decision to have our wedding at the beach was an easy one. Our first trip together was to the beach, we got engaged at the beach, and we would both like to live at the beach one day.

If you choose to stay for a week, check out some of the vacation homes. We recommend finding a Sunday-Sunday rental (most are Saturday-Saturday).
