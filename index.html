Our Story

How We Met

We met in 2012 through a meetup group, Triad Twenty-Somethings. Kristen set up a bowling event for the group and Bryan attended. He was one of the first people to arrive and we spent the majority of the night talking, even in the parking lot after everyone was done bowling for the evening. When Kristen left, she knew she wanted to get to know Bryan better. We hung out again together with the group that following weekend, then Bryan asked her on our first date that evening. We started dating on July 1, 2012 and have been inseparable ever since!

The Proposal


We took a trip to Duck, NC in May 2015 to celebrate Kristen's graduation from school and to spend a week together. We always try to have a week just to ourselves every year. One morning, Bryan asked if Kristen wanted to go watch and take pictures of the sunrise. It was chilly and windy that morning, but absolutely gorgeous. Bryan asked Kristen to close her eyes. When she opened them and turned around, he was on one knee with a ring in hand. On the sand was a heart with “Will you marry me?” written inside it. Of course, she said yes!

Why The Beach?

Choosing the beach as the place we were going to get married was not a hard decision for us. Our first trip together was to the Outer Banks and we got engaged at the Outer Banks. We also try to take at least a week together at the Outer Banks every year. We’re beach people if you couldn’t tell, and we would both like to live at the beach one day!
